TRAVEL & EXPENSES — ACME COMPANY
==================================

Acme Company's Travel & Expense (T&E) policy is designed to keep business travel efficient and cost-controlled while ensuring employee comfort on longer trips.

TRAVEL BOOKING
All business travel must be booked through TravelPerk, Acme's approved travel management platform. Booking outside TravelPerk requires prior Finance approval and may not be reimbursed. TravelPerk is accessible via the intranet or mobile app.

BOOKING GUIDELINES
- Flights: Economy class for all flights under 6 hours. Business class permitted for flights over 6 hours with VP approval.
- Hotels: Up to $250/night in most cities; up to $350/night in high-cost cities (NYC, London, Singapore, San Francisco). Exceptions require manager approval.
- Rail: Standard/Economy class. First class permitted for journeys over 3 hours with manager approval.
- Car rental: Compact or mid-size category. Collision damage waiver (CDW) must always be accepted.

PER DIEM
Meal expenses are reimbursed based on actuals up to the following daily limits (USD equivalent):
- US domestic: $75/day
- Europe: $90/day
- APAC: $85/day
Receipts required for individual expenses over $25.

EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Expenses are submitted in Concur within 30 days of the expense date. Receipts must be attached. Manager approval is required for all submissions. Approved expenses are reimbursed in the next payroll cycle.

CORPORATE CARDS
Employees with corporate Amex cards should use them for all business travel. Card limits and categories are pre-set; out-of-policy spending may be declined. Lost or stolen cards must be reported to Finance immediately.
