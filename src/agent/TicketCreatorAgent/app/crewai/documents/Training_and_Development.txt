TRAINING & DEVELOPMENT — ACME COMPANY
=======================================

Acme Company invests in continuous learning for all employees. The Learning & Development (L&D) function sits within HR and is responsible for mandatory compliance training, role-based technical learning, and leadership development programs.

LEARNING PLATFORM
All training is delivered through Workday Learning, integrated with LinkedIn Learning and an internal course catalog. Employees log in via SSO. Completion records feed directly into Workday performance profiles.

MANDATORY TRAINING
All employees must complete the following annually:
- Information Security Awareness (60 min)
- Anti-Bribery & Corruption / Code of Conduct (45 min)
- Data Privacy & GDPR Fundamentals (45 min)
- Health & Safety (30 min, region-specific)
Overdue mandatory training triggers automated reminders and is escalated to managers after 14 days.

LEARNING BUDGET
Each full-time employee has an annual L&D budget of $1,500 for external courses, certifications, and conferences. Requests are submitted in Workday for manager approval. Unused budget does not roll over.

CERTIFICATION SUPPORT
Acme fully covers the cost of job-relevant professional certifications (e.g., AWS/Azure certs, PMP, CPA, CISSP). Employees who leave within 12 months of completing a company-funded certification may be required to repay the cost on a pro-rated basis.

LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT
High-potential employees at the Senior Manager level and above are eligible for the Acme Leadership Academy, a 6-month blended program run in partnership with UT Austin McCombs School of Business.

MENTORSHIP PROGRAM
A structured mentorship program pairs employees with senior leaders for 6-month engagements. Applications open in January and July each year via the HR intranet page.
