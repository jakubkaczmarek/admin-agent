ACCOUNTS & ACCESS MANAGEMENT — ACME COMPANY
=============================================

Acme Company follows a least-privilege access model. All access to systems and data is role-based and reviewed quarterly.

IDENTITY PROVIDER
Microsoft Entra ID (formerly Azure AD) is the single identity provider. Employees authenticate via SSO for all internal and cloud applications. MFA (Microsoft Authenticator) is mandatory for all accounts.

ACCOUNT LIFECYCLE
- Provisioning: IT creates accounts from HR-approved onboarding tickets. New hire accounts are ready on Day 1.
- Role Changes: Access is updated within one business day of an approved change request from HR or a manager.
- Offboarding: Accounts are disabled within 1 hour of an employee's departure notification and permanently deleted after 90 days.

ACCESS REQUEST PROCESS
1. Submit a request in the IT Help Portal specifying the system, required permission level, and business justification.
2. Manager approval is required for all non-standard access.
3. Access to sensitive data (PII, financial, source code) additionally requires the Data Owner's approval.
4. IT provisions access within 2 business days.

PRIVILEGED ACCESS
Admin and privileged accounts are managed via CyberArk PAM. Privileged sessions are logged and audited. No standing admin access is granted; just-in-time elevation is used for all elevated operations.

ACCESS REVIEWS
All access rights are reviewed every quarter. Managers certify their team's access via the IT Portal. Uncertified access is automatically revoked after 7 days.
