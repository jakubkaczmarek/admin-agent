LICENSING & BILLING — ACME COMPANY
====================================

Acme Company manages software licensing centrally through the IT Procurement team in coordination with Finance. All software licenses are tracked in the Software Asset Management (SAM) system (Snow Software).

SOFTWARE LICENSE MANAGEMENT
- Volume licensing agreements are negotiated annually by IT Procurement.
- License assignments are reviewed every quarter; unused licenses are reclaimed to reduce cost.
- Microsoft 365 and other per-seat SaaS licenses are provisioned and de-provisioned automatically via Entra ID lifecycle workflows.
- Employees must not share software licenses or credentials under any circumstances.

COMPLIANCE AUDITS
Acme undergoes annual software license audits. The SAM system produces reconciliation reports comparing installed software against entitlements. Any shortfall must be remediated before the audit window closes.

VENDOR BILLING
- SaaS and cloud vendor invoices are received by Finance and matched against approved purchase orders in NetSuite.
- Unexpected or unrecognized charges must be reported to it-procurement@acmeco.com within 5 business days of the invoice date.
- Annual true-up invoices from major vendors (Microsoft, AWS, Salesforce) are reviewed jointly by IT and Finance before payment.

CLIENT BILLING
Acme bills clients on a monthly subscription basis for AcmeCloud licenses and on a time-and-materials basis for consulting engagements. Invoices are issued by Finance through NetSuite. Billing disputes from clients are handled by the Accounts Receivable team in coordination with the Client Success manager.

RENEWALS
License renewal reminders are generated by Snow Software 90 days before expiry and assigned to the relevant IT Procurement owner for negotiation.
