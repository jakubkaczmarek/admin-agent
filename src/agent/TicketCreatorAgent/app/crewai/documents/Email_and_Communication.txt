EMAIL & COMMUNICATION — ACME COMPANY
======================================

Acme Company uses Microsoft 365 as its unified communication platform. All business communication must be conducted through company-issued accounts.

EMAIL PLATFORM
- Provider: Microsoft Exchange Online (Outlook)
- Domain: @acmeco.com
- Mailbox size: 100 GB per user; shared mailboxes available upon request
- Email retention: 7 years (per legal and compliance policy)
- Spam/phishing filtering: Microsoft Defender for Office 365 + Proofpoint

INSTANT MESSAGING & COLLABORATION
- Primary: Microsoft Teams (chat, calls, video conferencing)
- Channels are organized by team and project; use of public channels encouraged over email for team discussions
- External guests may be invited to Teams with manager approval; guest access is audited monthly

ACCEPTABLE USE
- Company email and communication tools are for business purposes. Limited personal use is tolerated but must not interfere with work or violate policy.
- Sending confidential company data to personal email accounts is strictly prohibited.
- Employees must not use personal messaging apps (WhatsApp, Telegram, etc.) for business-sensitive communication.

EMAIL SIGNATURE
All employees must use the standard Acme email signature template provided by Marketing. Signatures are enforced via an Exchange transport rule for external emails.

OUT-OF-OFFICE & ARCHIVING
- Employees are expected to set out-of-office replies during absences of more than one business day.
- Mailboxes of former employees are converted to shared mailboxes for 90 days before deletion, accessible by their former manager.
