PROCUREMENT & PURCHASING — ACME COMPANY
========================================

All purchasing activity at Acme Company flows through the Procurement team to ensure cost control, vendor due diligence, and compliance with financial controls.

PURCHASING THRESHOLDS & APPROVAL
- Under $500: Manager approval via purchase request in NetSuite
- $500–$5,000: Department Head approval
- $5,000–$25,000: VP + Procurement review
- Above $25,000: CFO approval + Legal review for contracts
- Above $100,000: Board approval required (annual budget items exempt if pre-approved)

PURCHASE ORDER PROCESS
1. Employee submits a Purchase Requisition in NetSuite with business justification.
2. Required approvals are routed automatically.
3. Procurement issues the Purchase Order (PO) to the vendor.
4. Goods/services received are confirmed in NetSuite (3-way match: PO, receipt, invoice).
5. Finance processes payment per vendor payment terms (net-30 standard).

PREFERRED VENDOR LIST
Procurement maintains a Preferred Vendor List (PVL) of pre-vetted suppliers with negotiated pricing. Employees are encouraged to use PVL vendors to accelerate approval. New vendors require a vendor onboarding process: W-9/W-8, bank details, and a brief security/financial due diligence review.

PROHIBITED PURCHASING
- Personal purchases on company accounts are strictly prohibited.
- Purchases from relatives or entities with a conflict of interest must be disclosed and approved by the Ethics Committee.

CORPORATE CREDIT CARDS
Select employees hold Amex corporate cards for travel and incidental expenses. Card holders must reconcile expenses monthly in Concur. Unreconciled expenses after 30 days are escalated to the employee's manager.
