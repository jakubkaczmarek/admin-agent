ACME COMPANY — ORGANIZATION OVERVIEW
=====================================

Founded: 1995
Headquarters: 142 Innovation Drive, Austin, TX 78701, USA
Regional Offices: New York, London, Warsaw, Singapore
Industry: B2B Technology Solutions (SaaS, IT Managed Services, Consulting)
Employees: ~2,400 (as of 2024)
Website: www.acmeco.com

MISSION
-------
Acme Company delivers enterprise software and managed IT services that help mid-market businesses operate more efficiently and securely.
