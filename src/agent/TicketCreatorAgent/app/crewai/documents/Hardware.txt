HARDWARE MANAGEMENT — ACME COMPANY
====================================

Acme Company standardizes hardware to reduce support overhead and ensure security compliance. All company hardware is owned by Acme; personal devices may not be used to access internal systems unless enrolled in the MDM BYOD program.

STANDARD HARDWARE PROFILES
- Office employees: Dell Latitude 5540 (Windows 11) or MacBook Pro 14"
- Developers / Engineers: MacBook Pro 16" (M-series) + optional 27" external monitor
- Executives: MacBook Pro 14" + iPhone 15 Pro (company-issued)
- Reception / Shared workstations: Dell OptiPlex desktops with locked-down standard image

PROCUREMENT
New hardware requests are submitted through the IT Help Portal. Standard laptops are provisioned within 5 business days for existing employees and pre-staged for new hires. Hardware above $2,500 requires department head approval.

ASSET TRACKING
Every device is registered in the asset management system (ServiceNow CMDB) with a unique asset tag. Employees sign an Asset Acknowledgement Form upon receiving hardware.

REPAIRS & REPLACEMENTS
- Warranty repairs are handled directly with Dell/Apple via IT.
- Accidental damage once every 24 months is covered by company policy; additional incidents may be charged to the employee.
- End-of-life hardware (>4 years) is replaced on a rolling basis.

RETURN POLICY
All hardware must be returned to IT within 2 business days of an employee's last day. IT wipes devices using certified data-destruction procedures before re-imaging or disposal.
